about us

AutoBG Pro was built with one clear mission: to help car dealerships make their vehicle photos stand out. In a competitive market where first impressions matter, clean, professional-looking images can make all the difference. That’s where we come in.

I'm a solo developer with a passion for building practical, high-impact tools. After working with dealers and seeing how much time and effort goes into image editing, I created AutoBG Pro — a streamlined web app that removes backgrounds and lets you add studio-like custom scenes in seconds.

Whether you're listing one car or managing a full inventory, AutoBG Pro helps your photos look consistent, modern, and ready to sell — without needing Photoshop skills or extra staff.

If you're ready to level up your dealership visuals, you're in the right place.

Privacy Policy

We may collect basic information such as your name, email address, and dealership name when you create an account or contact us. Additionally, we may store the images you upload for processing, but only temporarily and solely to complete the requested task.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide and improve our services. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Uploaded images are processed and then deleted after a short retention period unless explicitly saved by you.
